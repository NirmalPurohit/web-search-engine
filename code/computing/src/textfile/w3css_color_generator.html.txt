The coastline with five villages: Monterosso, Vernazza, Corniglia, Manarola, and Riomaggiore is a UNESCO World Heritage Site. Monterosso Monterosso al Mare is located at the center of a small natural gulf, protected by a small artificial reef, in the Riviera of La Spezia. It is the northernmost village of the Cinque Terre. Vernazza Vernazza is another of the five towns in the Cinque Terre region. Vernazza is the fourth town heading north. It has no car traffic, and is one of the truest "fishing villages" on the Italian Riviera.
